Khinkali

Khinkali is a traditional Georgian dumpling filled with spiced meat (often a mix of beef and pork), herbs, and onions. These dumplings are known for their unique shape with twisted knobs at the top, which make them convenient to hold. Khinkali is usually boiled and served hot. The juicy filling and flavorful dough make them a popular choice in Georgian cuisine.

Croissants

Croissants are buttery, flaky pastries of French origin. They are made with laminated dough, resulting in layers that are both crisp and tender. Croissants are a versatile treat and can be enjoyed plain or filled with various ingredients such as chocolate, almond paste, or ham and cheese. They are often served for breakfast or as a snack.

Cherry Pie

Cherry pie is a delightful dessert made with a sweet pastry crust filled with tart cherries and sugar. This classic American dessert is often enjoyed during the summer when cherries are in season. The combination of sweet and tangy flavors in every bite makes cherry pie a popular choice for those with a sweet tooth.
